    Re: Side Menu Gone in Upgraded Version

    Without a backup of the database, you'll need to do a manual install, which is essentially setting up ATutor without running the db upgrade scripts (in fact do not run the installer/upgrade this time).

    Move you broken upgrade directory to a new location, or rename it.

    Unpack a fresh copy of ATutor 1.5.4 into the old location of the broken upgrade.

    Copy or move the include/config.inc.php file and the content directory from the broken upgrade into the fresh copy.

    That should be anoutgh to get you running. Depending on customiztions, modules, etc, there may be a few more steps to copy eveything over from the broken upgrade.

    We do have a troubleshooting/installation service available if you'd like us to repair things for you. See the services page:
    http://www.atutor.ca/services/index.php

    Turn on content navigation as the instructor under the Manage tab, then Student Tools>Side menu

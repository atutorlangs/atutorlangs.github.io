    This probably came from the contact instructor link in the Browse Courses list. No others should have received it. If it becomes a real problem you could manually remove the contact instructor link by editing it out of the browse courses theme template, though if its just the occasional spam, it may be worth just ignoring them. I suppose it depends on how important allowing potential students to contact a course instructor is.

  • 2011-05-19 09:24:29

    Re: Auto Enrolment course not added

    If its a private course, you'll need to approve the enrolment requests. Auto enroll just eliminates the enrolment step after registering.

    You may want to turn on the email notification for new enrolments, in course Properties.

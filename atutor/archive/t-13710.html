    Re: Theme Compatibility issue

    You can edit the theme.cfg.php file in the theme, changing the value of $_theme['atutor-version'].


    Have a look at the theme changelog for details of changes. The primary changes to watch for are the addition of encryption, and the return link for admins viewing courses.

    http://wiki.atutor.ca/display/atutorwiki/Creating+Custom+Themes

    In many cases you can delete the changed files from your theme to have them inherited from the default theme. Or you can look at the changed files and copy over the changes to your theme.

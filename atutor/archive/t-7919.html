  • 2006-05-04 17:43:33

    Re: Access management

    in Manage>Properties set your paid courses to be Private. Then enroll your Premium Users in those courses. You can also make course Private when you create them.

    re: auto-update, you'd need to write a script that checks you ecommerce program for payments. There isn't such a tool currently. An ecommerce module would be a nice feature for someone to create.

    Re: Access management

    There is no concept of a "premium user" in ATutor, or other LMSs as far as I know.

    After you've made the so called premium courses private, you can enroll your paid clients in those courses to give them premium access.

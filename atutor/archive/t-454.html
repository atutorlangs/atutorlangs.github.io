    Changing Numbering System of Activitiespages

    Greg

    I would like to make use of the internal numbering system to use:
    top level pages for Modules
    next level pages for Weeks of the Module
    next level pages for Activities for that week.

    Once created, is there an easy way to re-jig the numbers without going into the database.

    EG. After developing a week with 6 activities (1.1.1 - 1.1.6), an author comes up with another activity that should be second for the week. How can this activity become 1.1.2 and bump the current 1.1.2-1.1.6 to become 1.1.3-1.1.7

    thanks
    larry


  • 2003-09-22 09:11:43

    Move the activity

    On the edit screen for an activity page, choose from the "Move To" menus the place where you would like to insert it. The numbers will update themselves.

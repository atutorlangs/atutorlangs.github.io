    Hi all / Nerilab,
    I have found 'phantom' links in the SCORM and ecomm modules and these are generally caused by faults in each modules module.php file.

    The problems stem from 2 causes - if the file paths are incorrect or missing for the child links or in two instances of the SCORM mosdule on line 30&31 they are commented out.

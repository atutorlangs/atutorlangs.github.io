    Course Preferences

    You can set course preferences to limit what gets displayed (though this isn't working quite right in the current release). You could also capture a preference string from your own account listing in the atutor members table after setting your preferred ATutor configuration, and copy that string into the database query submitted when a user registers.

    Utimately though it is the learner who decided what tools are displayed, if they want to change their preferecne settings.

    In ATutor 1.4, you'll be able to modify templates to control what, and how, functionality is diplayed.

    separate applications

    ATutor and ACollab are separate applications that can run on their own, thus each has its own chat, forums, etc. Both apps can shared a login session which allows a person to login to ATutor and have access to ACollab via that ATutor session.

    Have students participate in group preparation of a paper or project, using the drafting room to share revision efforts, the library to collect resources for their project, and the calendar to schedule online meetings via the chat, record due dates or milestones for a project, or announce other group related happenings.

  • 2005-02-08 14:22:40

    Merging AColab and ATutor

    Because ATutor and ACollab are two separate applications, much like ATutor and PHPnuke, or ATutor and Mambo are separate applications, creating a utility that would merge the common features would greatly complicate the interaction between the two.

    Some day we do plan to recreate the various ACollab tools as part of ATutor, though I expect that will be a ways off. If some group were to fund such work it might get done faster ;-)

    Re: I cannot see more than three questions

    As far as I can tell the question bank is working okay.

    Look in the database table tests_questions and see how many exist there.

    If you are pasting from a Word doc, MS quotation marks will break data. In such cases delete the MS quote after pasting, then retype it manually.

    Also make sure you haven't selected a category of questions, as opposed to All Categories.

    The question bank is specific to a course, so you won't see questions created in other courses.

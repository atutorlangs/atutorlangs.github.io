  • 2017-01-23 15:48:17

    Re: ATutor Tracking Capabilities

    Mostly yes.

    Logins are tracked per course, not length of time logged in per student.

    Content usage is tracked, as a whole. Number of times a page is accessed, and cumulative time spent on pages

    Content usage per student, number of times pages are accessed and cumulative time spent on page is tracked

    Quiz answers tracked, for individuals, and by question item for item analysis.

    Not sure what you mean by location/bookmarking.

    You can also install the gamification module, which tracks a range of tool and content usage as well.

    http://wiki.atutor.ca/display/atutorwiki/ATutor+Gamification

    Re: Selling Atutor as a Service

    ATutor is quite customizable right out ot the box. The tools provided in an installation, and the look of the system are pretty much fully customizable. If you do need features added, we, and other developers, can be hired to create them. Depending on the nature of the features, they may become part of the base source code, in which case they become part of the public domain, or in many cases propriatary features can be built into modules or themes, in which case you can choose to maintain full ownership over those features.

    There are restrictions on selling ATutor itself, but not on services associated with it, or content created with it. We encourage service providers to offer such services. We only ask that if you as a servcie provider require support from us, that you purchase a support package. Its only fair that if we are helping you generate revenues, you contribute part of that revenue back to ATutor.

    Of course if you can manage ATutor without our help, you are under no obligation at all to pay for anything.

    As for the features you mention, all exist in ATutor, oir as addon modules

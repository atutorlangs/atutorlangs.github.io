    Re: Atutor Reqs. Besides Apache/php/mysql

    2000 users is a small number actually. You should not have any problems running ATutor even on lower end hardware. The only thing you might be concerend with is space, if you have a lot of content to upload into your courses, particularly video, and to a lesser degree audio files.

    If its just text and images, any hardward back to even an old P1 should work. Of course if you do want performance, something more modern would be needed. Any current low end system should be all you need for the number of users you have.

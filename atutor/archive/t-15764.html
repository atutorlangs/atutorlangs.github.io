    One at a time. But, if you are trying to delete the categories, just detete it, and it will be removed from all the questions.

    There have been reports from some Windows users of being unable to extract zip files from ATutor. It seem to be related to winzip on XP Pro. You could try another archive app like winrar. That said though, be careful editing backups directly though it can be done, its easy to break a backup.

    Re: Collecting users (Students) in Groups

    Hi Nathan,

    As of version 1.6.4, there're no system-wide groups. But I see two tools you might find useful.
    The first works within individual courses: [i]Manage[/i] -> [i]Groups[/i].
    The second is system-wide: [i]System Preferences[/i] -> [i]Auto Enrollment At Registration[/i].

    Please see the Handbook on the corresponding ATutor pages for further information.

    Re: General advice for a custom usage of Atutor

    Actually, our gradebook doesn't allow commenting on student progress; we didn't have such a requirement.

    As for the existing tools, there's no exact match for your requirements, which is why I suggested to just try some.

    I suppose that you want the comments to be private just like marks should be, right?
    If so, then [i]technically[/i] you can do that by, say, creating a group for each student and enabling a forum for that group (in the group settings). Thus, each student would see an additional forum, access to which would be restricted to himself and the instructor. As a bonus (or a drawback, depending on your needs), the student would be able to fully [i]discuss[/i] the marks using the forum engine.
    Of course, this solution isn't very scalable and would probably work well only for small number of students.
    Same trick can be done with blogs.

    Alternatively, you could have a look at the internal mail (Inbox link at the top right corner) and perhaps groups in the social network tool.

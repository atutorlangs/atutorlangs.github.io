    Automated tests

    Glad you solved the problem. Missed the version number when I was scanning through the thread this morn.

    FYI
    In 1.4 students could delete automatically marked test, which were intended to be practice type tests. We've changed that in the current version, so automated tests can no longer be deleted by students.

    Just a note of caution, online testing is a risky business unless an invigilator is present. It can be difficult to gauruntee the test taker is the person they say they are. Be aware of this if you are basing students' marks on their online test scores.

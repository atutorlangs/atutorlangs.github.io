    Re: e mail to users fom admin page does not work [SOLVED]

    I discovered what is issue. E-mail is not "sent" by ATutor itself. It is sent the underlying E-mail software (i.e. sendmail).
    If admin is defined in his/her account with E-mail address that is located on remote computer/server E-mailing software on the server may not be able to access it and thus, not send E-mail message.
    E-mail addres should be changed on /home/My Account and in teh list of users including admin's part in admin panel.
    In addition, on some servers there are quotas how much mails can be sent at once.
    So if your course. does have 50 students and quota is 30 students you may get into trouble.
    If you have such a problem please check those two things first.

    Thanks, vedran

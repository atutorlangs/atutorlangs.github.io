    Re: Content Created OtherLanguage

    You also can:
    1. Log in as an admin.
    2. Go to System Preferences -> Languages.
    3. Select "English" (or whichever language your site is in).
    4. Press "Edit".
    5. Change "Character Set" to the one used by Korean language.
    6. Click "Submit".

    It should work.

    Comparative Study on Virtual Learning Environments UPDATE

    Dear Friends,

    We have received reports that some users faced technical problems trying to fill out the online surveys prepared for our comparative study on virtual learning environments. After receiving these reports, the surveys have been examined and some bugs fixed, so that we hope no further problems will be experienced. As posted before, these surveys target three different kinds of e-learning platform users, and are located on the sites indicated below:

    a) Survey for Administrators

    http://canberra.ics.kagoshima-u.ac.jp/comp_study_LMS.php

    b) Survey for Teachers/Instructors

    http://canberra.ics.kagoshima-u.ac.jp/comp_study_LMS3.php


    c) Survey for Students

    http://canberra.ics.kagoshima-u.ac.jp/comp_study_LMS_5.php

    We will greatly appreciate your contribution to our study by filling out the questionnaires for any of the indicated roles. It is our belief that documenting as many and diverse experiences as possible will be important to build a more objective characterization of ATutor as learning platform.

    Thank you very much in advance for your kind support.

    Ramon Iriarte
    Dpt. of Information and Computer Science
    Faculty of Engineering
    University of Kagoshima
    JAPAN

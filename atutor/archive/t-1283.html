    PHP/Text Editor

    I'm not familiar with Dreamweaver's PHP editing features.

    We all use plain text editors. In Window, Editplus, and in Linux, Kate. There are also a number of good PHP editors available.

    We will be publishing a programmers guide in the near future and will be opening our CVS so your modifications can be added to the ATutor source. This will be happening in May 2004. We will require one significant code contribution as a preresquisite to access the CVS.

    I'm wondering how a dictionary would differ from the current Glossary. I'd suggest you hold off on the calendar. One will be added as a part of the ACollab integration. The ACollab addon for ATutor should be available mid April.

    I'd suggest documenting your planned modification, so we can provide guidance, and perhaps make some of the modification ourselves.

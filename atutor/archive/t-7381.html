    It was a missing "s" after a % in the translation. I corrected it in the online translation for future translations.
    I corrected it on my system by finding (in table "AT_language_text" in the ATutor database) the "enrol_messagenew" and putting an "s" in place (after the first %).
    Now the e-mail with request for enrollment also tells wich course the enrollment is meant for.

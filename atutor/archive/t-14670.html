    Re: some problems with language

    The ????? is probably the result of content not being converted to UTF-8 when you upgraded, or it was converted but you are using a windows-1250 Farsi language pack.You should be using a UTF-8 version of the Farsi language pack.


    From what you have described, it sounds like the conversion utility (a third party application) may be having trouble with conversion of Farsi to UTF-8. I suggest you try exporting courses from the old installation, using the UTF-8 conversion module to convert the content, then importing the courses back into a fresh installation of ATutor.

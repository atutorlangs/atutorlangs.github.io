    Re: fsockopen() warnings

    Hi,

    When you select "Yes, send this information to atutor.ca.", step7 will send us some information for statistic collection. This warning message simply tells you that it has failed to send us that information, it could be a config problem on the PHP settings, or it could also be a timeout, namespace problem with the ATutor server.

    Having all that said, it will not affect your ATutor system.

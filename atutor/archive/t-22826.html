    Re: AContent limit size course

    You can adjust that setting in the file
    include/constants.inc.php

    Note though that as lesson sizes gets larger, they become increasing more likely to create problems when content is passed from system to system. Many systems will have limits on the size of files that can be transferred, so a lesson more than 100mb might not import. We set 100mb as a recommended maximum size for a lesson for that reason. If they get larger, you might consider breaking them down into multiple smaller lessons.

    Video content quite often results in large lessons, so to keep your lessons at a more manageable size, and so video content does not end up using up unnecessary space when content is replicated, we recommend locating video on YouTube, and may Vimeo, or some other video hosting service, then linking them into your content.

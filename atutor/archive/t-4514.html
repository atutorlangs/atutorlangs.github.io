    SCORM like

    What you want to do is some what like a SCORM lesson.

    An early version of SCORM player will be introduced in the coming June release. Though it won't do quite what you have described, it will allow you to sequence content based on some action.

    What you want to do is about a year away in ATutor. You could hire a programmer (even one of ours) if you wanted to speed up development.

    You might take a look at the Reload SCORM player and SCORM Editor.

    http://www.reload.ac.uk/scormplayer.html
    http://www.reload.ac.uk/editor.html

    Content created with the Reload Editor will import into ATutor.

    Or look for a SCORM 2004 compliant system, which I do not believe exists yet in open source. A few commercial system support it.

    Re: Email Configuration problem can't receive ATutor email.

    Assuming Sendmail is working on your Linux server, you should not need to do anything. I still think it has to do with the spam filters on the public email sites. Mantis may get through, but it does not mean ATutor mail can get through. Check the filters on these sites, and add mail from ATutor as trusted.

    Talk to your ISP if it continues to be a problem. If you have an email address from someplace other than these public sites, or know someone who does and would be willing to help with a test, try that.

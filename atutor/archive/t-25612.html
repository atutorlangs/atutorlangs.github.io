  • 2015-10-22 09:13:59

    Re: Sidemenu at the right side

    Probably float the side-menu to the right. Find this style in the theme's styles.css file.

    #leftcolumn {
    float: right;
    margin: -10px 5px 0;
    overflow: hidden;
    width: 21em;
    }

    There may be a few other adjustments, like moving the toggle to the right as well.

    Re: Update 1.6 -> 2.1

    Hi,

    No, unless you know what you are doing. If you simply copy the database over, it will not have the new tables nor any changes we made over the couple of versions.

    The best approach is to download 2.0.1 to say,
    docs/atutor_201

    while you have your old one at say,
    docs/atutor_16

    Then go to your browser and type in
    http://<your host>/docs/atutor_201

    It should pops up a screen asking you to install or upgrade, click upgrade. Then proceed with the screen instruction.

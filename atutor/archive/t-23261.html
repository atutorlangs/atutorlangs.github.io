    Re: How do I remove browse courses from Atutor 1.6.1

    There's a "course browser" setting in System Preference. If its not there (I don't recall when we added it) you can remove the browse courses link by removing its reference in $_pages[AT_NAV_PUBLIC] array of include/lib/menu_pages.php.

    You should probably get upgraded soon. We are no longer supporting the 1.6 series.

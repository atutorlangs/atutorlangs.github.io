    I'm not really sure what you are trying to do. Nothing is fixed. You can modify pretty much anything in a theme, and they will all take a logo if you want to add one.

    I'd suggest experimenting.


    ...and, you should not be editing the default theme. Other themes rely on it, so if you break it, you may end up breaking other themes. Instead take a copy, and work with it. Or better, read the theme designer documentation in the handbook.

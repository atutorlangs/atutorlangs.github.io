    HELP:Warning on login page.

    the error msg would suggest that your HD is full.

    or rather, the partition that PHP uses to save temporary session information is full.

    it's usually /tmp/ but from your error message it would suggest that it's storing the session files in /var/lib/php4

    you might want to change that to /tmp/

    IMS Support Coming

    We've built an IMS content packaging import/export utility for the upcoming release. AICC is not in our plans, though SCORM is, probably for a spring 2004 release (to include LOM, IMS Simple Sequencing, and IMS Test Interoperability).

    If you can provide an argument for including AICC support, that would provide significant utility over what IMS/SCORM offers, we'd like to hear it.

    I' actually using a LMS like i told before ,which seems to be the most used here (may be i'm wrong) , and in my actual work we (where i work) have planned to ask our courses providers (external societies) to make them build their courses using AICC only.

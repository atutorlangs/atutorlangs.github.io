    Re: can't find Instructor Tools

    Create an instructor for yourself, then as the admin assign that user as the instructor for the course under courses>properties.

    Then enroll the old instructor (who would now be an unenrolled student in relation to that course) and assign privileges to that student, to give him/her limited instructor features. Leave out the privileges you don't want the instructor to have access to.

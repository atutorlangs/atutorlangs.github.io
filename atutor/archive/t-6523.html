    About ATutor

    1. In terms of developers, how many different roles do you have? For instance, is there a release manager or test manager? Is there a chief architect?

    [green] Roles are fairly fluid. Depending on the task, we all take on multiple roles. There is a "chief architect", a lead programmer, and graphic design coordinator, as well as general programmers... [/green]

    2. Does the University of Toronto use ATutor? If so, how many users can we count?

    [green]Yes, but only minimally. UofT has had a WebCT license for several years. The move to ATutor will be slow. There are several small pockets of groups using ATutor at UofT now, but I could only guess how many users are on these systems. [/green]

    3. How many core developers does ATutor have?

    [green]five (though that varies up and down from cycle to cycle) [/green]

  • 2010-03-17 08:53:03

    Re: Gradebook question

    Hi,

    For ATutor tests, the scores are saved in AT_tests_results. When these scores are updated into gradebook at gradebook page "Update ATutor Marks", they are either updated in as raw score, or converted to a grade if the grade scale is selected. For external tests, the raw scores are saved only if no grade scale is selected for the test. Otherwise, the imported scores are converted and saved as a grade and the original scores are discarded.

    You don't have to disable the grade scales. As long as you don't select grade scale for your tests, they are not in use and harmless.

    Hope this would help.

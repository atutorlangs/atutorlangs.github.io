    Re: Default theme appears on all except one course.

    I'd guess you have category themes turned on, in which case you can reset the category to the same as the other courses in a course's Properties under the Manage tab.

    Or, you can disable all other themes in the admin's System Preferences>Themes

    This may be preferred if you don't want students choosing their own theme.

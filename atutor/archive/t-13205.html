    Re: Test Manager Problem

    It's Manage -> Tests & Surveys. It's not called a "manager" in the program itself.

    There are 3 simple steps for creating a test:
    1. Create an empty test, specifying its properties.
    2. Create questions in the Question DB.
    3. Add the questions to the test. For that, on Tests & Surveys page you should select a test, click Questions button and then click "Add Questions".

    In general, I'd suggest you to read the ATutor Handbook in your installation or at http://help.atutor.ca/instructor/index.php (look for "Tests & Surveys" there).

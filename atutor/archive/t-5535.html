    javscript is not imported

    for content packages, information in the head of HTML pages is stripped away, including JS.

    You can try pasting the JS into the body of a content page after it has been imported.

    Do not confuse SCORM content packages with SCOs. If you are importing SCOs you need to use the Packages tool.

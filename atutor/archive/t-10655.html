    My guess it has something to do with the way mime types have been configured with the webserver, and possible interaction with users' browsers and settings. IE 7 has introdcued a whole new bunch of issues, so I would not be surprised if it was IE7 users who are experiencing the problem. Perhaps have you sys admin look into mime configuration. Or, perhaps an Apache upgrade would resolve the problem.

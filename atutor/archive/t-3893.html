    Check Enrollment Manager

    Make sure the students are in the Enrolled list in the Enrollment Manager, rather than the Unenrolled list. If you are running a private course you need to approve enrollment requests by moving them from the Unenrolled list to the Enrolled list. Students will recieve a notice of enrollment when you do.

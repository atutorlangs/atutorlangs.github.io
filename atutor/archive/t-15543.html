    Re: Updated themes?

    You may need to adjust the older themes to work with newer versions of ATutor. The ones you are referring to were developed by someone else, and probably were not updated for the current version. You can look the theme change log in the ATutor Wiki (which is down at the moment) to see what has changed, and make adjustments to the theme if necessary by editing the files.

    hrrp://wiki.atutor.ca

    Or, you can edit the theme's theme.cfg.php file, changing the value of $_theme['atutor-version'] to match your ATutor version. Then zip the files back together and import the theme bundle as usual.

  • 2005-04-21 09:11:23

    fixable

    I'm sure it is fixable. It would be a matter of going through the tracker scripts and finding instances where these calculation are made, and perhaps eliminating the first record as the script loops through the tracker table, or using the first record as a base line for calculating durations that follow.

    It looks like the problem is that the first calculation does not have a base time to subtract from the timestamp listed in the record to come up with a duration.

    The other possibility is to set the first record yourself, logging in as a student, clicking on a content page, then quickly logging out. The calculation made after that will not be exact, but they will be very close.

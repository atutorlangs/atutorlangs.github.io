    the bounce.php and course id does the trick.

    Normally you can right click on the module link in the tabs across the top, or the icons on the home page and save its location, then paste that location into a content page. You can onoly do this within the course the FAQs belong to. You cannot link from within one course to another course for security reasons.


  • 2007-02-13 14:42:50

    Re: public url

    The only way to get into a specific page in a course (aside from the index.php page) is by adding a second argument called "p", which is the page you want to redirect to.

    example:
    /bounce.php?course=10&p=/forum/list.php

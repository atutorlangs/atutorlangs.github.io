    Re: Is polish translation moving on ?

    If you are planning to translate on your own system, you may run the partial language package you can download from the translator page after logging in, through the UTF-8 conversion utility, linked from the translations page.

    http://atutor.ca/my/
    http://atutor.ca/atutor/translate/index.php

    Once you have finished translating on your own system you can export then attached the language pack to a message in the translation forum.

    If you are translating on the atutor.ca site, continue translating in the ISO charater set, and convert it to UTF-8 after you are finished.

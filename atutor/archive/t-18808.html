  • 2009-10-01 16:21:22

    Re: Two instructors

    You can enroll the other person into the course (Manage -> Enrollment) and give him/her all the privileges (using Privileges button there).
    That way, you'll give to that person practically the same abilities that the instructor has.

    Further information: http://help.atutor.ca/instructor/enrollment_privileges.php

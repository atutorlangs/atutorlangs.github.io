    Related Topics

    The Related Topics tool was created for this purpose. Manually creating links within content pages is possible, though those links would break if you moved the content. The related topic links are maintained when you move backups, though a standard for creating related topics does not exist, so they will only work in ATutor.

    For interoperability purposes, links to other pages within your content should not be used, perhaps a flaw of the current standards. We recognize the need for such links, which lead us to create the Related Topics tool.

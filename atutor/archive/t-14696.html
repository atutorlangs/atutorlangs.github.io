    Re: student history

    As long as courses and tests remain on the system, a student can continue to access them through MyCourses. This assumes courses are not reused from semester to semseter, but instead copied into a new course for each semester.

    There isn't a specific tool in ATutor for doing this, but you could use something like the Mahara ePortfolio module where students could record their own "careers"

  • 2008-09-15 02:52:33

    No such option for security reasons

    There's no option to enable HTML in forum posts because that would give rise to cross-site scripting (XSS) vulnerabilities. See http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cross-site_scripting .

    Forums only support some most commonly used formatting (font weight, colors, smileys etc.).

    I'd suggest you to have a look at the EWiki module for ATutor at http://www.atutor.ca/atutor/modules/index.php . I believe it supports much more formatting options, and, depending on the intended use, may suit your needs.

    Re: No such option for security reasons

    Type a URL into the forums. It will get turned into a link. You can not post html to the forums for security reasons. You can not open a link in a new window from the forums without html.

    Wiki's use wiki language rather than html. See the documentation with the wiki for details about wiki language.

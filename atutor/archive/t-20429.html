    Re: Some problems upgrading to ATutor 2.0 from 1.6.4 (jpeg, multimedia, import)

    First I'd recommend a version of php less than 5.3. Current higher versions are somewhat unstable, and could be the root of the problems you are experiencing.


    Bypassing validation is a "use at your own risk" feature. If a package won't validate, you need to talk to the developers of the tools that created the package etc.

    What do you mean by "jpeg formats are not recognized?" If you look at a course's properties screen, at the bottom where you upload a course icon, is jpg listed as an option, or is it just gif and png?

    Re #2 not sure what's happening there. I can't reproduce the problem.

    Re: Cannot login to admin or other accounts

    If you attempt to login and fail 5 times, the login screen is disabled for an hour. A security feature to prevent repeated attempts to figure out someones password.

    As the admin you must manually reset your password in the database. Find the row in the "admins" table, retype the password and save as type "sha1".

    For other users click "Forgot your password" on the login screen.

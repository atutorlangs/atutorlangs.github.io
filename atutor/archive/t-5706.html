    Preference Tab

    Preferences are now accessed through the Preferences Tab on My Start Page. Some of the preference options have been removed in ATutor 1.5 and 1.5.1

    Most preferences are now controlled through themes. Create themes with the position, display, and text or icon features modified, then make those themes available to users through the Them Manager.

    Given the admin can log into courses to manage them, the functionality you need is there, albeit with an extra step to access courses.

    You can not merge admin and instructor rights. A direct admin login is not something we would consider adding.

    The other possibility is to have all your courses owned by one instructor, and use role and privileges in the enrolment manager to assign instructor privilieges to others.

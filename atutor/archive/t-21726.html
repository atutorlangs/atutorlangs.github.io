  • 2011-01-28 11:50:16

    Re: Glossary in AContent?

    No plans to add glossary to AContent, and probably won't unless it becomes part of the Common Cartridge standard. We probably could remove glossary terms when exporting, but maybe it would be better to strip them out when imported into AContent. At least with glossary items in, when moving content from ATutor to ATutor or from ATutor course to course, the they are preserved. Something to think about for a future release.

    AContent is intended to be used with systems other than ATutor that support common cartridge or content packaging standards, so if we add glossary, glossary items will end up breaking when imported into other systems.

  • 2008-12-02 12:26:33

    Re: test question categories question

    Questions and question categories are associated with courses. For smaller installations it might be desirable to have questions in the question db available across all courses, but in most cases making questions public to an entire ATutor system is not desirable.

    If you want to make modifications to this, adding a "public" property to test questions, and perhaps question categories, might be a useful addition.

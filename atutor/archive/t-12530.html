    Re: Question of Tests & Surveys

    First you create your test by clicking "create test".
    Then click "Question Database" from within "create test".
    Write your questions. Then you put you questions into the order you want them in and save them. (The program puts them in alphabetical order.)
    Then name the test, assign a date and a group if you have any groups, and save it. You're done

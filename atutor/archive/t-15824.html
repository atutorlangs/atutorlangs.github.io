    Re: Integrating Articulate software with Atutor? Also.. SCORM?

    I can't say for certain that Articluate content will work with ATutor. Product conformance with the standards are not equal.

    I might suggest you try a demo of articulate and upload a package to our demo site, or to a test course on your own installation of ATutor.

    Be sure not to confuse content packages with sharable content objects (SCOs), the latter of which needs to be imported into the ATutor SCORM Packages module.

    You might try the Reload editor. Its free.

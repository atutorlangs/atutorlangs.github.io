    base_href required

    You could try setting AT_FORCE_GET_FILE to FALSE in include/config.inc.php if your content directory is located within the directories of your ATutor installation. If the content directory is outside your installation, then get.php is required to retrieve files from your cource content directory. If you are linking to things other than content, you will need to use a ful URL.

    See the Plone theme for ATutor 1.5. It has the menu on the left. It's not too difficult to move.

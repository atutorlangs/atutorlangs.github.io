    Re: Password disappears during login

    The password disappearing is normal. It gets encrypted before sending it to the server.

    This problem generally occurs when working with a custom theme or an older one that hasn't had the encryption scripts added to the login template.


    There's some discussion here that may help.
    http://www.atutor.ca/view/7/14863/1.html

    Or, use the search to look for "login encryption"

    Also, if you've installed ATutor by uploading file-by-file, rather than uploading the bundle and unzipping it on the server, that might also contribute to the problem.


  • 2010-03-21 13:17:06

    Re: Password disappears during login

    The solution!

    FYI: In case anyone else has this problem in the future; apparently ATutor uses "third party cookies" in the login process. When I changed my settings to allow third party cookies I was able to successfully login.

    Thanks for your help, it was a supposition posed in the thread you pointed to that led me to try this.

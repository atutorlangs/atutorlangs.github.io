    1.2 to 1.3

    courses you export from within 1.2, you will be able to import into 1.3 easily. the only change has been to move the import/export section from the control centre to the course's tools page and renamed to "Backup".

    we also added content packaging which lets you (or students, if you let them) download entire content packages for offline viewing or importing into other courses.

    Re: Not printing page 1 but page 2 prints fine.

    hi! thanks for finding this.

    here's the fix:
    -open /themes/include/header.tmpl.php
    -search for the line that contains "$this->current_date" (around line 253)
    -in its containing div, replace "float:right" with "text-align:right"

    that should do it. it appears that IE is defining the float-area differently so when our print stylesheet hides the sub-nav area, it is including the page text.

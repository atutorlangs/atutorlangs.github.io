    Re: Is it possible to direct go to the content anchor?

    The content anchor is there for assistive technology users, who will notice the link to it in the upper left (a bypass link) when their assistive technology begins reading the page, or if they tab into the page (it's the first link). It can also be accessed uing the alt-c accesskey.

    There is no admin option to automatically jump the the anchor, though if you're creating links to your pages, you could include the anchor in the URL. Jumping to the content top when the page loads would be disruptive for most people so its unlikely we'd make it an option. At least not an admin option, but perhaps a content author option associated with specific pages.

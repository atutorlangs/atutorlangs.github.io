    Re: Can a 2007-era ATutor export IMS packages?

    Common cartridge did not exists until 2008 officially, so you may or may not be able to export CCs. You should be able to export IMS Content Packages, and if Moodle has implemented CC right, you should be able to import those.

    Go to Manage>Content>Import/Export

    You could upgrade to a newer version of ATutor to export out CCs. Or, perhaps import a backup into the demo site (if its not too large) and convert it to a CC there.

    Re: Can't open folder and CC license

    That's the way folder now work. They are simply a toggle to open and close a selection of topics for student. A folder editor for instructors. It was required as part of the recent Common Cartridge implementation. When you restore a content package, or a backup, all top level pages with child pages are converted into a folder, then that page is converted into a child of itself. You can potentially go back and edit the folder names if needed. Any new content will be created with the folder toggles and sub pages.

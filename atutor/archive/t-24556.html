    Re: Logging issue

    There was a password encryption step added in ATutor 1.6.1, which converts all text passwords to a 40 character SHA1 encrypted string. Check the passwords in the DB to be sure they have been converted.

    You can manually reset passwords in the db by saving them in SHA1 format.

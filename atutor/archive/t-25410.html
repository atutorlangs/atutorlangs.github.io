    Re: Glossary: Can it be imported from another course?

    Yes.

    Create a backup of the first course in Manage>Backups
    Upload the backup to the new course in Manage>Backups.
    Select the backup once uploaded and click Restore
    In the restore options choose just Glossary (and append or overwrite)

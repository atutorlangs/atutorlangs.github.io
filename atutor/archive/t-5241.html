  • 2005-07-25 17:43:51

    Manage>>Content

    Logged in as an instructor, under the Manage Tab, click Content. Add sub-content there, or by clicking Add Sub Page in the Shortcuts box on any content page while logged in as a course instructor. You can move pages under the Properties tab of the Content Editor.

    See the ATutor Handbook, linked from most tool pages as help, and in the ATutor footer area, for more information about creating and managing content.

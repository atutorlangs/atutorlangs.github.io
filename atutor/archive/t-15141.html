    Re: Permitting students to view only a specified set of courses

    Because browse course is available to students without logging in, there's no way currently to prevent them from seeing other courses.

    You can limit access though. Make the courses private (Manage>Preferences), and have students enroll. Non-enrolled students will not be able to access private courses

    Re: Bulk Upload Student Enrollment

    Hi,

    The feature is called the "Import course list", and can be found in your ATutor handbook or the online help at http://help.atutor.ca/

    Log in as instructor->manage->enrollment

    Then on the top right, there is the handbook icon, underneath the "Enrollment" menu, there is the "Course List"


    If you can't find it, here is a direct link to our online help:
    http://help.atutor.ca/instructor/enrollment_course_list.php?en

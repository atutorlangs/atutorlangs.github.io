    Re: Import issue: Unable to see photos

    The trouble is these packages are created with an older version of the packaging standard than ATutor supports. It uses IMSCP 1.1. Packaging was introduced into ATutor at IMSCP 1.1.2.

    I'm not certain, but you may be able to import the package into another autoring tool that does support the older standard, and convert it to the newer one. I believe the Reload SCORM/IMS editor will let you do this.

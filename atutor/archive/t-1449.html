    Copyright must remain intact

    You can change the logo, but as a condition of the GPL the copyright notice must remain intact. You can remove the ATutor HowTo line if you want.


    Adding your own copyright notice using the header/footer editor should have no affect on "real estate" since it appears after your content and can be a single line of text.

    Create a link

    You could however, copy the link to the SCO into an announcement, forum message, or content page etc.

    something like:

    http://www.atutor.ca/atutor/demo/tools/packages/scorm-1.2/learner_view.php?org_id=13

    Right click and "save link as" to copy the link location from the Packages screen to the clip board, then copy it into a page [ctrl-c] . Terms will vary depending on the web browser being used.

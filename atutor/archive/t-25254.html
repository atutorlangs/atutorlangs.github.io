    Re: Lateral Menu on right

    There's nothing in the patches that would affect the position of the menu.

    In ATutor 2.2, all the themes have the menu on the left. I can't say exactly what you'd need to do to moving it to the right, but start by finding the side-menu in the theme include/header.tmpl.php file, and moving it, probably into the start of the footer.tmpl.php file. You'll need to experiment a bit until you find the right place.

    https://github.com/atutor/ATutor/blob/master/themes/blumin/include/header.tmpl.php#L324-L331

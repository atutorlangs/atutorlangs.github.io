    Re: 35 students kicked out frequently & extremely slow page loads

    My first suspicion is that cookies from an older version of ATutor are conflicting with those in the new version.

    To resolve the problem, users must dump any ATutor related cookies from their browser.

    I'd suggest testing this with one student who knows his/her way around iPad browser settings, to see if it fixes the problem. For an iPad, go into Settings>Safari then find Cookie options and delete cookies from there.

    If it does not help, try accessing ATutor with a different browser.

    Re: Breadcrumbs as student

    zesar1980, can you find out the breadcrumb nodes that are skipped on the student's page are "content directories" or "contents".

    You probably have known that the "content directory" is the non-content in the course content structure, whose only purpose is to hold the sub-contents or sub-content-directories. Clicking on the "content directory" takes the course creator to "edit content directory" page and only does expand/collapse directory for students.

    So, if the skipped breadcrumb nodes are content directories, this is an expected behaviour, otherwise, if any contents are skipped, report a bug @ http://atutor.ca/atutor/mantis/view_all_bug_page.php

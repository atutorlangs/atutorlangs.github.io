  • 2009-01-14 10:42:58

    Re: Hyperlinking between pages in ATutor

    Be careful creating links in content pages. If you export content the links will break when you re-import it back into ATutor or another system.

    You could use the "Related links" feature under the properties tab of the content editor, to create links to other pages.

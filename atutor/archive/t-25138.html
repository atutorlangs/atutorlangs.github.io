    Re: Some more things I would like to remove or switch off for students

    You can disable browse and "things current" in system preference, networking and calendar in the module manager.

    Preferences cannot be remove with a setting, though you could dig into the code and remove it. Start by looking at the header.tmpl.php files in themes, include/lib/menu_page.php, and the include/header.inc.php file to trace where the preferences tab gets created. Removing Preferences may have other consequences, I'd probably recommend again removing it.

    That should get you going.

    AContent 13 Beta

    A Beta version of AContent 1.3 is now available. This version adds admin content authoring, LTI 1.1 Provider features for linking AContent into other systems, structural templates for generating a variety of pedagogical course structures, and page templates to quickly format page layouts.

    See the AContent changelog for additional details.

    http://atutor.ca/acontent/change_log.php

    Users are encouraged to try the demo, and download and install the beta for testing, and provide feedback on adjustments needed or bugs encountered.

  • 2008-07-21 12:02:10

    Re: Hosting Company Question

    These days most providers have the requirements to run ATutor. We don't endorse any provider in particular, we've just had a few happy customers report back on their experience with SiteGround, not to say that other providers don't provide the same level of service. And SiteGround does provide free installation, which draws many people to them.

    If you do decide to go with another provider, we can potentially do the installation for you, if the provider doesn't provide that service. A one time Installation and Troubleshooting ticket is what you'd need.

    There is no downside to running on other hosts that we might identify. The list just happens to be those who have reported their service, or that we have come across while on the Web.

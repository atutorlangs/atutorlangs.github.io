    Re: Common Cartridge import error

    I can confirm that CC importing on the demo site works okay, so this is likely a problem with foreign characters in your content. This often occurs when content is copied and pasted from MS Word for instance. The quote characters often do not copy properly, and end up breaking a cartridge or content package.

    Try exporting a single page, then a folder, then a larger unit to determine where the break occurs. Then go through the content, delete any quote marks, then retype them.

    ATutor Social is Here

    It's finally here. ATutor has implemented the OpenSocial standard, and added a social networking module to ATutor. The current development version has been installed in the atutor.ca demo site for those who would like a preview. Look for the Networking tab.

    http://www.atutor.ca/atutor/demo.php

    Over the next Week or so, a beta will be released and be available through the ATutor module installer for anyone using ATutor 1.6.2 (or greater). Over the next month or so, we will be gathering feedback, bug reports, feature suggestions etc. before the final release coming May/June 09.

    Post your comments etc to the forums:
    http://www.atutor.ca/forums
